✨ New Features Designed

Manager Allocation Requests

Managers can request products from warehouse/stores with business justification. Regional manager approves with one click.

Real-Time Transfer Notifications

Store-to-store transfers trigger instant WebSocket notifications. Both stores see status updates in real-time.

Customer Shipment During Purchase

SA captures shipping address at checkout. BOH gets notification when production completes. Auto-generates UPS label.

SA Client Tracking

SA can search any customer and see complete journey: Purchase → Production → Ready → Shipped → Delivered.

Manhattan "PICKED" Status

When BOH scans RFID tags before shipping, items automatically marked as "PICKED" in Manhattan WMS.

RFID Scan Confirmation

BOH uses Zebra RFID40+ scanner to confirm items before shipping. Updates all systems automatically.
