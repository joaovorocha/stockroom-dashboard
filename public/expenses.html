Employee Discount Policy: 40% discount, max retail value €2,500 per calendar year (or local equivalent). Employees are responsible for not exceeding this threshold. Discount must be applied in-store by manager using employee’s Suitsupply email. Manual discounts, remote authorizations, and freelance/contractor discounts are not allowed. Unauthorized or unassigned transactions will be flagged for investigation.
