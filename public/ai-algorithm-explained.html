How Our Fair Rotation Algorithm Works

Everyone does everything - no favoritism, just fairness

⚖️ Core Principle: Equal Rotation

Our AI doesn't play favorites. It analyzes the past 90 days of assignments and ensures everyone rotates through all zones, fitting rooms, and shifts fairly.

Why rotation? Because everyone should experience all areas of the store. No one gets stuck in the same zone forever. No one always gets the "easy" shift. Fair is fair.

🎯 No Complex Skill Grading

We deliberately don't grade employees on skills. Why? Because:

  • Everyone at Suit Supply is capable and professional
  • Skill grading creates bias and favoritism
  • Learning happens through experience, not labels
  • Fair rotation ensures everyone develops all skills

Bottom line: If you're on the team, you're trusted to do the job.

How the Algorithm Works (Step-by-Step)

1

Load Historical Data

The AI looks back at the last 90 days and counts:

  • How many times each employee was assigned to each zone
  • How many times they worked each fitting room
  • How many times they had each shift (morning/evening)
  • How many times they had each lunch time

Example: John has worked Zone A 12 times, Zone B 8 times, Zone C 15 times in the last 90 days.

2

Calculate Fairness Scores

For each employee, the AI calculates a "fairness score" for each zone/room/shift:

  • 0.0 = Never assigned (most fair to assign)
  • 0.5 = Assigned moderately
  • 1.0 = Assigned most often (least fair to assign)

Example: John's Zone Fairness Scores

  • Zone A: 0.80 (assigned 12 times - too many!)
  • Zone B: 0.53 (assigned 8 times - okay)
  • Zone C: 1.00 (assigned 15 times - way too many!)
  • Zone D: 0.00 (never assigned - time to assign him here!)

Result: AI will assign John to Zone D because it's the fairest choice.

3

Make Assignments (Fair First)

The AI assigns zones, rooms, and shifts by prioritizing the LOWEST fairness scores:

  • SA Employees: Assigned 2 zones (least-assigned zones first)
  • Fitting Rooms: Assigned to each SA (fair rotation)
  • BOH Employees: Assigned shifts and tasks fairly
  • Lunch Times: Rotated fairly across all employees

The algorithm ensures no zone is over-assigned and everyone gets a fair rotation.

4

Manager Reviews & Approves

The AI generates a DRAFT assignment. It's NOT automatically published.

Managers can:

  • Review the AI's suggestions
  • See the fairness score (0-1, higher = more fair)
  • Make manual adjustments if needed
  • Approve and publish the final gameplan

Why? AI is smart, but managers know the team. Human judgment is always the final say.

5

Learn & Improve

Every assignment is logged in the database. Over time, the AI gets better at:

  • Balancing rotations more evenly
  • Avoiding patterns (e.g., same person always closing)
  • Identifying when someone hasn't been assigned to an area in a while

The longer we use it, the fairer it gets.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can the AI override manager decisions?
No. The AI only generates draft suggestions. Managers always have the final say. If a manager manually changes an assignment, the AI logs it and learns from it.
Q: What if someone is sick or on vacation?
Managers can mark employees as "off" before generating the AI draft. The AI will skip them and distribute tasks among available employees.
Q: Does the AI consider employee preferences?
Currently, no. The AI prioritizes fair rotation over preferences. However, we can add a "soft preference" feature in the future where preferences are considered as a secondary factor (after fairness).
Q: How do I know if the assignment is "fair"?
The AI calculates a Fairness Score (0-1). A score above 0.90 means assignments are very evenly distributed. You'll see this score when reviewing the draft.
Q: Can I see why the AI assigned someone to a specific zone?
Yes! (Coming soon) We're building an "Explain Assignment" feature that shows the historical data and fairness scores that led to each assignment.
Q: What if the AI makes a mistake?
Managers can reject the AI draft and create assignments manually. The AI will learn from manual assignments and improve over time.
Q: Is my data private?
Yes. All AI processing happens locally on our server. No employee data is sent to external AI services. Assignment history is stored in our secure PostgreSQL database.

🎓 The Philosophy Behind This

Why did we build this?

Managers spend 20-30 minutes every day manually assigning tasks. It's tedious. It's easy to forget who did what last week. Unconscious bias creeps in.

Our solution: Let AI handle the tedious math of rotation fairness. Let managers focus on coaching, strategy, and customer experience.

The result: Fair assignments in seconds. More time for what matters. Happier team.
