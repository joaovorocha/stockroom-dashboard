Barcode / QR Code Scanner

Scan with USB scanner or use camera

Scanner Behavior

  • Data Matrix: Copies data to clipboard and opens Inventory System
  • PSUS / Order QR Code: Opens Order Status (PSUS + 8 digits, or 8 digits starting with 04)
  • Ignores any data before "PSUS" and after the 8 digits
  • Press Enter or Tab after scanning to process
